Our mortgage calculator generates your monthly payment for 10, 15, and 30-year fixed-rate home loans. We use the following formula to generate your monthly payment:
(Principal * APR) / (1 - (1 + APR) -[# months])

Remember the 28/36 rule! Spend no more than 28% of your pre-tax income on housing, and no more than 36% on your total monthly debt.

How to Use:

Enter your loan principal and annual percentage rate (as a whole number), then select one of the aforementioned loan periods. Then, click the "Calculate" button to see your monthly mortgage payment!
